Colors Can Trigger Deep Childhood Memories

When human beings see a color (or even simply hear the name
of a specific color), it calls to mind several associations—
objects, moods, even temperatures—that were established as
far back as childhood, says historian and symbologist Michel
Pastoureau, author of the book Blue: The History of a Color.
